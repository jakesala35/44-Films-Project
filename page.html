44 Films: Who We Are

44 Films is a collaboration between the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications and Syracuse University Athletics. Newhouse students produce short documentary films based on the most notable stories in Syracuse sports. 44 Films takes Syracuse fans, students and alumni behind the scenes to experience their favorite players, coaches and traditions like never before. Our goal is to tell the incredible stories of the Syracuse student athletes and sports figures beyond the world of sports. The Syracuse sports experience just got even better, and we're just getting started. Go Orange!
